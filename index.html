The maker

Every scent is
a story

Hi, I'm Tobe' — the maker behind Terracotta Gypsy. What started as a passion project became something I couldn't stop. I fell in love with the alchemy of fragrance — the way a single scent can transport you somewhere instantly, or make a space feel entirely yours.

Every candle is small batch, hand poured in my studio on Washington's Olympic Peninsula. I obsess over fragrance combinations until they feel exactly right. The scent names aren't random — they're little stories. I want you to smell them and feel something.

— Tobe'
